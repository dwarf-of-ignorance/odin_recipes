Marinate for steak

The recipe title says steak, but this is a well-balanced marinade that you can easily use on chicken, pork, and vegetables. Note that Nicole adds honey, which adds flavor and caramelization.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • half cup soy sauce
  • 1/3 cup red wine vinegar
  • 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 3 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • grounded black pepper
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion sliced

Steps

  1. Mix all the ingredients in a medium bowl
  2. Marinate your meat
